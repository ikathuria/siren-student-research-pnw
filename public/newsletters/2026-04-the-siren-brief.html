4. Research Highlights

VibeGen: Designing Proteins by Motion

Instead of static 3D folding, this AI designs proteins based on how they vibrate and move at a molecular level.

Why it matters: This allows for "dynamic" medicine that only activates when it "feels" the specific mechanical vibration of a target cell.

World2Act: World Models as Action Planners

A new framework that trains AI to master complex physical tasks by breaking them into smaller, reusable "latent" skills.

Why it matters: A major leap for AI Robotics by allowing models to "plan" multi-step actions in the real world more efficiently.
